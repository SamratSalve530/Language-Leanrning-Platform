Basic Pronunciation

Understanding how to pronounce Marathi letters and words is crucial for effective communication.

  • Vowels are pronounced similarly to their sounds in English and Spanish.
  • Consonants are similar to English but may have aspirated forms (e.g., ख (kha), घ (gha)).
  • Practice saying simple words to get familiar with the sounds.
  • Listen to native speakers to improve your pronunciation.

Phrases

Learn common Marathi phrases.

Introductions

  • माझं नाव [तुमचं नाव] आहे. (Majh naav [your name] aahe.) - My name is [your name].
  • तुम्ही कसे आहात? (Tumhi kase aahat?) - How are you?
  • तुमचं भेटून आनंद झाला. (Tumcha bhetun anand zala.) - Nice to meet you.

Travel

  • हे कुठे आहे? (He kuthe aahe?) - Where is this?
  • मला मदतीची गरज आहे. (Mala madatichi garaj aahe.) - I need help.
  • किती पैसे आहेत? (Kiti paise aahet?) - How much does it cost?

Emergency

  • पोलिसांना बोलवा! (Polisanna bolwa!) - Call the police!
  • मला डॉक्टरची गरज आहे. (Mala doctorchi garaj aahe.) - I need a doctor.
  • हे माझं आवडतं ठिकाण आहे. (He majh avadtam thikaan aahe.) - This is my favorite place.
