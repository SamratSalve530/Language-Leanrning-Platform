French Alphabet

Learn the basics of the French alphabet.

Letters

Basic Pronunciation

Understanding how to pronounce French letters and words is crucial for effective communication.

  • Vowels are pronounced as in Italian, but nasal vowels are unique to French.
  • Consonants are similar to English, but some letters are silent (e.g., the last consonant in "parle").
  • Practice saying simple words to get familiar with sounds.

Basic Phrases

Learn common French phrases for everyday conversations.

Greetings

  • Bonjour - Good morning / Hello
  • Bonsoir - Good evening
  • Merci - Thank you
  • Au revoir - Goodbye

Introductions

  • Je m'appelle [Your Name] - My name is [Your Name]
  • Je viens de [Your Country] - I am from [Your Country]
  • Comment ça va? - How are you?
  • Ça va bien, merci - I'm fine, thank you

Basic Questions

  • Où est...? - Where is...?
  • Combien ça coûte? - How much does it cost?
  • Quelle heure est-il? - What time is it?
  • Parlez-vous anglais? - Do you speak English?
