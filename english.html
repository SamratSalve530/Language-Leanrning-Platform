Basic Pronunciation

Understanding how to pronounce English letters and words is crucial for effective communication.

  • Vowels are pronounced as in Spanish.
  • Consonants are similar to English but may have aspirated forms (e.g., "p" in "pat" vs. "p" in "spat").
  • Practice saying simple words to get familiar with sounds.
