Basic Pronunciation

Understanding how to pronounce Hindi letters and words is crucial for effective communication.

  • Vowels are pronounced as in Spanish.
  • Consonants are similar to English but may have aspirated forms (e.g., ख, घ).
  • Practice saying simple words to get familiar with sounds.

Phrases

Learn common Hindi phrases.

Introductions

  • Mera naam [Your Name] hai. (मेरा नाम [आपका नाम] है) - My name is [Your Name].
  • Aap kaise hain? (आप कैसे हैं?) - How are you?
  • Mujhe khushi hui aapse milkar. (मुझे खुशी हुई आपसे मिलकर) - Nice to meet you.

Travel

  • Yeh kahan hai? (यह कहाँ है?) - Where is this?
  • Mujhe madad chahiye. (मुझे मदद चाहिए) - I need help.
  • Kitna paisa hai? (कितना पैसा है?) - How much does it cost?

Emergency

  • Police bulao! (पुलिस बुलाओ!) - Call the police!
  • Mujhe doctor ki zarurat hai. (मुझे डॉक्टर की ज़रूरत है) - I need a doctor.
  • Yeh meri pasandida jagah hai. (यह मेरी पसंदीदा जगह है) - This is my favorite place.
